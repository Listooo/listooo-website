🔐 Privacy Policy

Listooo Package name: com.adriano.listooo Last updated: 30.12.2025

This policy applies to the Android app “Listooo” and the website listooo.ch.

Data Controller

Adriano Gennuso Email: support@listooo.ch

What data is processed?

  • Account information (Google login): name, email address, profile picture
  • App content: lists, products, notes, reminders
  • Images & camera: product photos (locally and/or Firebase Storage)
  • Location (optional): for location-based reminders / geofencing
  • Device/log data: OS version, error logs

Purpose of processing

  • Synchronization and backup (Firebase Firestore)
  • Image storage (Firebase Storage)
  • Authentication (Firebase Auth)
  • Push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • Error diagnostics and app stability improvements

Legal basis (EU/EEA)

Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR – contract performance Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR – legitimate interest Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR – consent (e.g. location, notifications)

Data sharing

No data is sold. Processors include: – Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage, FCM) – Google Play Services

Data retention

Data is stored as long as a user account exists or until deletion.

User rights

Access, rectification, deletion, restriction, data portability, withdrawal of consent, and right to lodge a complaint.

Account and data deletion

Accounts can be deleted directly in the app. For full deletion requests: support@listooo.ch → Deletion within 30 days.

Children

This app is not intended for children under 13 years.

Changes

This policy may be updated. The current version always applies.

Contact

Questions: support@listooo.ch
